About Fusion Towers B Wing

Fusion Towers B Wing is a modern residential tower located in Bhoirwadi, Hinjewadi Phase 3, Pune. The society is home to 110 apartments, managed by a 13 member elected Managing Committee and supported by professional security, housekeeping and maintenance teams.

We are committed to maintaining a safe, well-governed and community-driven environment with transparent communication and digital-first operations for all members and residents.

Society Rules & Guidelines

Core guidelines to ensure a peaceful, safe and well-maintained living environment.

General Discipline

  • Maintain low noise levels between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM.
  • Segregate wet and dry waste and handover only to authorised housekeeping staff.
  • Do not park vehicles in non-designated areas or in front of other flats.
  • No storage of inflammable material in parking or common areas.
  • Any commercial activity from flats requires prior written approval from the Managing Committee.

Renovation & Interior Work

  • Work to be carried out only between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM (Monday to Saturday).
  • Contractors and workers must be registered at the security gate with valid IDs.
  • No structural changes that impact beams, columns or load-bearing walls are permitted.
  • Lift padding is mandatory for movement of materials and furniture.
  • Debris to be disposed of outside the society premises by the flat owner/contractor.

Common Areas & Pets

  • Pets must be on leash in common areas and lifts; owners are responsible for cleanliness.
  • No playing of cricket or high-impact sports in parking areas and driveways.
  • Children should be supervised in the play area and should avoid using lifts alone.
  • Do not place personal items permanently in corridors, staircases or lift lobbies.
